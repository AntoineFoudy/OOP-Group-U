## Algebra Basics
Algebra is the branch of mathematics dealing with symbols and rules for manipulating them.

## Key Topics
- Variables and Constants
- Expressions and Simplification
- Linear Equations
- Quadratic Equations
- Polynomials

## Applications
Algebra is used in physics, computer science, economics, and everyday problem solving.

## Summary
Mastering algebra is essential for progressing in higher mathematics and STEM fields.
