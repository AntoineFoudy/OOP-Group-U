## Calculus Overview
Calculus studies change and motion, including derivatives and integrals.

## Key Concepts
- Limits and Continuity
- Derivatives: rate of change
- Integrals: area under curves
- Differential Equations

## Applications
Calculus is widely used in science, engineering, economics, statistics, and optimization problems.

## Summary
Understanding calculus enables modeling and solving complex real-world problems involving change and growth.
