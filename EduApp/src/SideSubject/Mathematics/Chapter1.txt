## What is Mathematics?
Mathematics is the study of numbers, quantities, shapes, and patterns.
It provides tools for logical reasoning, problem solving, and analytical thinking.

## Core Concepts
- Numbers: integers, fractions, decimals
- Algebra: expressions, equations, functions
- Geometry: points, lines, angles, shapes
- Logic & Proof: reasoning, deduction, induction

## Why Study Mathematics?
Mathematics is fundamental for sciences, engineering, finance, and technology.
It develops critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

## Summary
Mathematics helps us understand the world, analyze data, and solve real-world problems efficiently.
